## Key Takeaways

- **Personal liability exposure** - Owning property personally exposes all your assets to lawsuits
- **LLC structure** - Creates liability shield between property and personal assets
- **Trust benefits** - Provides estate planning + privacy + asset protection
- **Multi-entity approach** - Wealthy families use holding companies, operating companies, and trusts together
- **Privacy** - Entity ownership keeps your name off public records
